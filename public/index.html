Help & FAQ

How does NanoShare work?

NanoShare temporarily encrypts and stores your file on our local server. It generates a 7-character code that can be used on any device to securely download the file.

Does it work without internet?

Yes! If you are on the same Wi-Fi network or using a Mobile Hotspot, the transfer happens directly over your local network.

Are my files secure?

Files are strictly temporary. They are automatically and permanently deleted from the server the moment they are downloaded, or after 15 minutes of inactivity.
